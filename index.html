About Me

Hello! I'm Richard Gabel, a Computer Engineering student passionate about hardware and software technologies.

I am driven by my curiosity and love for problem-solving and learning, leading me to take every opportunity I can to expand my skill set. I achieve this through numerous side projects across different fields of Computer Engineering, including Arduino, SystemVerilog, Full Stack development, Game Design, AI, and more.

One of my favorite things about Computer Engineering is the ability to work on both hardware and software aspects of a project, leading to many opportunities to showcase creative innovations and solutions. The far-reaching impact of technology on our daily lives begets unique and dynamic ways of applying my skills to help create new experiences for the public.

I have always been an altruist, trying to help those around me, and I see my work as an extension of that. I want to use my skills to help impact the world, whether big or small. My work in the various organizations I am a part of helping to provide technology education to underprivileged schools in my area and collaborating with my High School to encourage more students to pursue a college education have demonstrated this, and I don't plan to stop my impact anytime soon.

I am seeking a Summer 2026 internship relating to Computer Engineering fields.
